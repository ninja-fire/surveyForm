FASHION INDUSTRY IMPACT

Everyday, more and more people become aware that fashion industry is one of the most polluting in the world, and that need to change. Here, you can learn some clue ideas and answer a little form to discover the impact of your clothes on the people and the planet.

DID YOU KNOW THAT...

Over 100 billion clothes are sold around the world every year.

Fashion industry creates more than 1.2 billion tonnes of greenhouse gases each year. Its impact is greater than international flights and maritime traffic combined.

When caring for our synthetic clothing by machine, it is estimated that 500,000 tonnes of plastic micro particles are released into the ocean each year, the equivalent of more than 50 billion plastic bottles.

Making a t-shirt requires the equivalent of 70 showers and 285 showers for a jean.
